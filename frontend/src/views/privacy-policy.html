Trancendence's Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2023-10-02

At Transcendence, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains the data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. By using our service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.


1. Information Collected

We collect the following information when you sign up or fill out a form:

  • Email address
  • Pseudonym
  • Gmail account


2. Methods of Collection

The information is collected when you sign up or fill out forms on our platform.


3.Use of Data

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Improving our services
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Communicating important information about your account and our services

We do not share your data with third parties.


4. Data Sharing

We do not share or transfert your personal information to third parties. There are no subcontractors or partners with acces to your data.


5. Data Security

The security of your data is paramount to us. We use an encrypted database and have implemented a WAF (Web Application Firewall) and ModSecurity to protect your information from unauthorized access.


6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for a period of 6 months from your last login. If your account remains inactive during this period, your information will be deleted from our database.


7. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.


8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We do not collect data via cookies or any other tracking technologies.


9. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated to our users via email.
